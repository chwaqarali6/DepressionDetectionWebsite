Depression Analysis in Adults

In the past few years a great number of psychological researches have been carried out on depression. The purpose of this project is to detect depression from the face expressions of an individual. The input to the system is given in the form of videos which are further manipulated for diagnosis. Proper diagnosis of this serious disease is undoubtedly most important part of its cure. This tool will aid psychologists and psychiatrists in detecting depression. We can certainly say that it is a decision support tool but not a decision-making tool. To give a clearer understanding of the project a report has been made available for users.
